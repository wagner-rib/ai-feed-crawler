Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

DeepTrendLab does not collect personal data. We do not require registration or login. Standard server access logs (IP address, browser, page visited, timestamp) are retained briefly for security and performance monitoring, then discarded.

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. No persistent cookies are set by DeepTrendLab itself.

Third-party content

Article images are served directly from the original publishers' CDNs. Embedded videos (YouTube, etc.) may set their own cookies when played. We have no control over third-party cookie behaviour.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics (no personal data collected, no cross-site tracking).

Original content

All article content displayed on DeepTrendLab belongs to the original publishers. We link to the original source on every article. If you are a publisher and wish your content to be removed, contact us at hello@deeptrendlab.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be noted at the top of this page.
