About DeepTrendLab

DeepTrendLab is an independent news aggregator dedicated to the AI and machine learning ecosystem. We monitor over 50 of the most respected sources in the field — research labs, tech publishers, academic blogs, and industry newsletters — and surface the most relevant content in one place, updated every 2 hours.

What we cover

From foundational research papers and model releases to tools, policy debates, and real-world applications. Our categories span LLMs, computer vision, robotics, AI policy, MLOps, and more.

Our sources

We curate from publishers including Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Hugging Face, ArsTechnica, TechCrunch, MIT Technology Review, The Verge, and many others. All original content and rights belong to the respective publishers. See the full source list →

How it works

Articles are fetched automatically from RSS feeds every 2 hours. Full article content is extracted and cleaned from the original source pages. Topic tags are generated to help with discovery. No content is modified or summarised — what you read is what the original publisher wrote.
